Delicious Lasagna Recipe

Who doesn't love a delicious homemade lasagna?

We know we do, but we also know that making said lasagna can be very time consuming

That is why we came up with this easy to make, DELICIOUS homemade lasagna!

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Onion and garlic
  • Tomato products
  • Sugar
  • Spices and seasonings
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Cheeses
  • Egg

See? Nothing too absurd or hard to find! And making it will be just as easy!

Step-by-Step

  1. Make the meat sauce
  2. Cook the noodles
  3. Make the ricotta mixture
  4. Layer the lasagna according to the recipe instructions
  5. Cover with foil and bake!
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving

Doesn't know how to do the layering? Don't worry! We got your back!

How to Layer your Lasagna

  1. Meat sauce
  2. Noodles
  3. Ricotta mixture
  4. Mozzarella slices
  5. Meat sauce
  6. Parmesan cheese
  7. Repeat the layers, then top with the remaining Parmesan!

And that's it! You got your homemade lasagna ready to serve for your family and friends, Bon Apetit!
