Homemade Pasta Recipe

There is nothing that fits more with a romantic dinner than some pasta

We know it, the Lady and the Trump know it too!

But why use the artificial untrustworthy pasta sold at the supermarket when you can make your own?

Don't worry! It's super easy and we will be with you in every step of the way!

Ingredients

  • All-purpose flour
  • Eggs
  • Olive oil
  • Salt

Yes, it is just these four ingredients. I told you it would be easy!

Now, let's get to making the pasta!

Step-By-Step

  1. Make a nest with the flour on a clean work surface
  2. Add the remaining ingredients to the center of the nest
  3. Mix everything gently with your hands
  4. Knead until it becomes smooth and cohesive!
  5. Shape it into a ball and wrap it in plastic wrap
  6. Let it rest for about 30 minutes
  7. Cut it into 4 slices
  8. Use a rolling pin (or your hands!) to flatten it into an oval disk
  9. Run it through the widest setting of your pasta maker
  10. Fold the dough into a rectangle and then run it on your desired thickness
  11. Then finally, cut and cook it!

Yes, it is this simple, now you can make your own pasta for that romantic date you wanted so much!
